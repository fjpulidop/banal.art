# Persona: The Content Creator

> "I need scroll-stopping content that's funny AND aesthetic enough for my feed"

## Profile

| Field | Value |
|-------|-------|
| **Name** | "Lucia" — The Content Creator |
| **Age** | 18–30 |
| **Occupation** | Social media enthusiast, micro-influencer, or marketing student |
| **Tech savviness** | High — uses multiple content creation tools daily |
| **Devices** | Mobile-first (iPhone), occasionally desktop for editing |
| **Platforms** | Instagram Stories/Reels, TikTok, WhatsApp, Twitter/X |
| **Spending** | Will pay for tools that save time or improve content quality |
| **Languages** | EN/ES — posts in both depending on audience |

## Behaviors

- Always looking for the next viral content format or trend
- Curates her feed carefully — only shares content that matches her aesthetic
- Takes multiple photos of the same object to find the best angle before uploading
- Screenshots and saves app outputs that look good enough to post
- Follows trends: if "AI museum plaque" goes viral on TikTok, she wants to be early
- Compares output quality across different AI tools before committing to one

## Value Proposition Canvas

### Customer Jobs

| Type | Job |
|------|-----|
| Functional | Generate a visually polished, shareable image from a mundane photo |
| Functional | Create content that works in Instagram Stories format (9:16) |
| Functional | Produce multiple pieces of content quickly (batch content creation) |
| Social | Post something that gets likes, comments, and shares |
| Social | Be seen as creative and witty by followers |
| Social | Start a trend or participate in one early |
| Emotional | Feel proud of the content she creates — it reflects her personal brand |
| Emotional | Experience the dopamine hit of engagement (likes, shares, DMs saying "this is hilarious") |

### Pains

| Severity | Pain |
|----------|------|
| Critical | Output image looks low-quality or amateur — can't post something ugly |
| Critical | No native share functionality — has to screenshot and crop manually |
| High | Watermarks or branding that clashes with her aesthetic |
| High | Can't customize the output (font, colors, layout) to match her feed |
| Medium | Rate limits that prevent creating multiple pieces in one session |
| Medium | Audio/video content is harder to create than static images |
| Medium | No way to add her own branding or handle to the output |
| Low | Desktop-only tools — she creates content on her phone |

### Gains

| Impact | Gain |
|--------|------|
| High | Museum plaque image in 9:16 format — ready for Instagram Stories without editing |
| High | One-tap share to Instagram/WhatsApp/TikTok via native share sheet |
| High | Visually stunning output that looks professional and on-brand |
| Medium | Download as high-res PNG — no quality loss from screenshots |
| Medium | Audio narration she can use as voiceover for Reels/TikTok |
| Medium | Bilingual output for reaching different audiences |
| Low | Subtle watermark (banal.art) that's tasteful rather than intrusive |
| Low | Trend potential — the museum aesthetic is unique enough to go viral |

## Key Insight

> Content creators don't just want funny — they want **funny AND beautiful**. The museum plaque format is the killer feature: it's a ready-made, aesthetically pleasing, share-optimized content piece. No other AI humor app produces output this polished and Instagram-ready.
